Description:
The event will be a simple model making competition. Students will have to prepare either of the following: i) A soft solution(App/Program) ii) A hard solution (Real-life models that can be used to demonstrate their ideas) for any of the many problems faced today by the society. The problem could be from any of the following disciplines:

  • Agriculture
  • Transport
  • Water Conservation
  • Education
  • Environmental Pollution
  • Health and Hygiene

Contest format :

The contest will have a single round, where the participants will display their ideas and explain how their model helps solve a societal problem. The models will be judged on the impact they create, possibility of implementation in real life, and overall cost of the solution.

Other details :

  • Students must develop the solution/model on their own and only use open source third party libraries.
  • Students must not use an entire open source project and present it.
  • The solution must be plausible.
  • Solutions will be judged on:
  • Severity of the problem solved
  • Simplicity of the solution
  • Efficiency of the solution
  • Vitality of the dependencies
  • Thinking process behind the solution
  • Electrical components must be assembled by the students themselves.
  • In case of cardboard models, the students must make it themselves
