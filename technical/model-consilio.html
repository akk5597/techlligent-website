Contest format :

  • ROUND 1: The participants will have to make a 3D model from the cardboard puzzle set provided. Time Duration – 1.5 hour
  • ROUND 2: The participants will have to make plan and elevation of the model on drawing sheet. Stationary is to be brought by the participants. Only sketch pens along with drawing sheet will be provided. Time Duration – 1.5 hour
  • ROUND 3: The participants will have to present their model in front of judges for 3 to 4 minutes. They will be allowed to surf on Google for information.

    • Other details :

    • Registration Fee: 100 Rs
    • Number of Participants: Atleast 2 and atmost 4 students per team
      • Judging Criteria:
      • On the basis of above 3 rounds, marks will be given
      • Judges decision will be final
      • In case of any disputes, the coordinator reserves all rights to disqualify the participants.
