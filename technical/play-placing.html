Description:
Play Placing is a mock placement drive where participants will go through the gut wrenching process of placement from technical tests to interviews.

Contest format :

  • It is an individual event.
  • It will test the technical skills of IT/ CS students.
  • It will be conducted in 3 rounds:
    • Apt2Aptitude: It will be a MCQ round testing the mental abilities, data interpretation, logical reasoning and analysis. ▪ Participants crossing a given cut off will go to round II.
    • CodeZone: It will be a Coding round to test the coding skills of participants in C or C++ ▪ Only top 20 to 25 participants will appear for round III.
    • TechInterview: It will be a technical interview round with an appropriate panel to test the technical skills and fundamentals of participant (the questions will be put considering the year the student is currently in)
