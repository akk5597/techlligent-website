Description:
Fandom quiz is based on famous television series plus movies. We are looking up to find our hard core fans.

Contest format :

  • Each group consists of maximum 4 students.
  • Organizers have all rights to disqualify the team if caught cheating or using foul words.
  • Any team or associate causing problems or misbehaving with the organizing team will be disqualified.
  • Every round will be an elimination round.
  • The quiz will focus on TV series and movies listed below:
      • Movies:
    • Harry Potter Series
    • All Marvel movies (The Hulk series, Iron Man series, Avengers series, Captain America series, Spiderman Homecoming and Antman)
      TV series:
    • FRIENDS
    • Game of Thrones

Round Details :

  • Round 1: MCQ

    Participants will have to solve MCQ based questions in the given time limit.

  • Round 2: Rapid Fire

    Participants will be given a time limit of 90 seconds and they should be answering maximum number of questions in the defined time limit.

  • Round 3: Taboo Cards

    Each team will be given a set of 15 cards. The participants are expected to guess the character, movie or TV series mentioned.

  • Round 4: Secret Round

    This round will be disclosed on the spot.
