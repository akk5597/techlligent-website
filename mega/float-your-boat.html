Description:
Make a boat that can float and also carry weight. The models are to be made on the spot.

Contest format :

  • Round 1: On Spot Model Making


    Teams will have to make a boat model with propeller mechanism for the material provided. Problem statement will be given on the spot. (Participants are allowed to bring scissors)

      Material
    • Icecream sticks
    • Fevicol
    • Cutter
    • Rubberband

  • Round 2: Elimination Round


    The model will be tested on the basis of time required to cover the given distance. Teams will be shortlisted.

  • Round 3: Load Testing


    Qualified models will be tested and evaluated on the basis of ratio (self weight of the boat / weight taken by the boat)

Other details :

Judging Criteria:

  • On the basis of 3 rounds, marks will be given
  • Judges decision will be final
  • In case of any disputes, the coordinator reserves all rights to disqualify the participants.
